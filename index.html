About Us

Born from Hustle. Built on Trust.

La Familia Media started with grit, vision, and a belief that small businesses deserve serious branding without Fortune 500 pricing.

Sergio Castillo founded La Familia Media in 2012 out of a belief that small businesses deserve the same quality branding as major companies.

Over 12 years, the agency has grown into a full-service creative partner serving businesses across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach.

Hablamos español. We understand South Florida’s bilingual market and help brands connect clearly across communities.
