About Us

Built from hustle, vision and family.

Designing brands with purpose and longevity.

La Familia Media was built through years of creativity, resilience and real-world experience in branding, promotion, design and digital development.

What started as an entrepreneurial journey evolved into a creative company focused on helping businesses communicate better, look stronger and grow with intention.

Our Timeline

The story behind La Familia Media

From nightlife promotion and graphic design to branding, web development and digital reinvention, this is the path that shaped our company and our creative identity.

August 2006

Our founder, Sergio Castillo, has his entreprenurial spirit awoken, as he starts a side business with brother, Isaac Castillo, for marketing and promotion of nightclubs and events.

September 2008

Due to the global financial crisis, Sergio loses his job at the bank. His side business is now forced to be his only income. Starts to make plans on scaling up the business.

April 2009

Our first sister company is founded in 2009 under Goodlife Miami, LLC. The company will focus on marketing, nightclub/event promotion, and artist development.

February 2010

We noticed the need for graphic design to market and promote events, such as flyers, business cards and graphics. Sergio enrolls in Florida Career College for Graphlc & Website Design.

January 2011

Partnered with a congomerate of entreprenuers, Sergio opens a new office in Southwest Miami, meant to be a headquarters for all things branding.

April 2012

Sergio along with partner, the late Chris 'Blaze' Rosales, officially start a new company called La Familia Media, LLC. La Familia is meant to focus on graphic design and website development services.

August 2014

Due to health conditions, our co-founder, Chris "Blaze" Rosales tragically passes away at the age of 24.

January 2015

La Familia Media partners with the Creative Complex, a design agency in Hialeah, FL. Sergio is mentored by fellow entrepreneur, Alex Miranda.

November 2018

After 10 years, Goodlife Miami, LLC is absorbed by La Familia Media, focusing on graphics, web design, and all things printing.

March 2020

As the world stops due to a global pandemic, we decide to Rebrand and reinvent ourselves. Going all digital, making it easier for clients to work in a modern age.

2021 - Present

We continue to serve our clients after 10+ years, bringing our repertoire and experience to our existing and new clientele.
