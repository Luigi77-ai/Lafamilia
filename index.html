Our Story

Born from Hustle. Built on Trust.

La Familia Media didn't start in a fancy office. It started with a side business, a financial crisis, and an entrepreneur who refused to quit.

La Familia Media is built for the companies that power South Florida.

Sergio Castillo founded La Familia Media in 2012 out of a belief that small businesses deserve the same quality branding as Fortune 500 companies — without the Fortune 500 price tag.

Over 12 years, we've grown into a full-service creative agency serving businesses across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. We've worked with everyone from corner restaurants to medical societies — and we bring the same dedication to every single project.

Hablamos español. We understand that South Florida is a diverse, bilingual community — and we're one of the few agencies that can serve you equally well in either language.
